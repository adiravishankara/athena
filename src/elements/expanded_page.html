StarFab Accelerator's CIAT program is seeking startups in IoT, Big Data, Cloud Computing, and Industry 4.0 for a 4-month mentorship opportunity starting in April. Selected small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs) and early-stage teams will receive guidance in product development, networking, marketing, and fundraising, with a unique chance to collaborate directly with leading Taiwanese enterprises. The program involves a multi-stage selection process, including application submission, initial interviews, a demo pitch, and a final Demo Day where startups can connect with venture capitalists. Ultimately, the accelerator aims to foster innovation and business partnerships within the cloud computing and IoT sectors.
